MÔ TẢ CÔNG VIỆC

Established in 2016 in Hanoi, 1C Vietnam operates as a subsidiary of 1C company – the leader of Russian and Eastern European business solutions market. There are over 3000 companies in Vietnam using 1C solutions in their everyday business operation and 1C Vietnam partnership network actively expanding through the major cities across the country.

To help expanding our business, we have an immediate opening for the position of Event Marketing Executive.

Job Description:

• Organizing and supervising internal online and offline events, such as Seminar, Workshop and Webinar

• Researching and planning for external events/expo

• Working with colleagues to execute Marketing activities with partners and supporting branding activities

• Working closely with Marketing team to execute and implement marketing communications strategies and plans.

• Doing market research to assess current trends to enhance brand awareness

• Preparing and optimizing marketing materials

• Planning and proposing new marketing initiatives to strengthen engagement with target audiences

• In-depth knowledge of market research data, data analysis and statistics

• Enthusiastic, dynamic and creative

• Excellent capabilities of writing and speaking English

• Modern marketing mindset with good interpersonal skills
